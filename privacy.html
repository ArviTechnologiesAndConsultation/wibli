Privacy Policy

wbli (“we”, “us”) provides tools to send WhatsApp template messages and track delivery analytics.

Data we process

  • Business identifiers you connect from Meta (WABA ID, phone number ID).
  • Contact data you upload (name, phone number) and campaign metadata (template name, language, status).
  • Message delivery/read statuses via Meta webhooks.

How we use data

  • To deliver WhatsApp templates you initiate and to show analytics.
  • To secure and operate the service (logs/monitoring).

Sharing

We share data only with infrastructure processors necessary to provide the service (e.g., Meta/WhatsApp APIs, AWS S3/DynamoDB). We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Contact lists and message logs are retained for your account until you delete them or request deletion.

Security

Data is stored on AWS (S3/DynamoDB). Access is restricted and audited.

Your choices

You can request export or deletion of your data at any time. See Data Deletion.
